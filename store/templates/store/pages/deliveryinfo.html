Order Processing

Orders placed on Cladly Fashion are processed on business days (Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays). Processing typically takes 1 business day before dispatch.

  • Orders placed before 12:00 PM IST on a business day are processed the same day.
  • Orders placed after 12:00 PM or on weekends are processed the following business day.
  • During sales events or festive seasons, processing may take up to 2–3 business days.
  • Custom, altered, or made-to-order items require an additional 3–5 business days before dispatch.
  • A dispatch confirmation email with your tracking link is sent as soon as your order ships.
03

Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, you will receive an SMS and email containing your tracking number and a link to monitor your shipment in real time.

  • Track your order at any time via My Orders in your Cladly account dashboard.
  • Real-time GPS tracking is available for Express and Same-Day deliveries.
  • If your tracking link shows no movement for more than 48 hours, please contact our support team.
  • International tracking updates may be delayed by up to 24 hours at customs checkpoints.

Our Packaging

At Cladly, packaging is an extension of the product. Each order is carefully folded, wrapped in signature tissue paper, and sealed in our premium black-and-gold branded box.

  • All packaging materials are 100% recyclable and sustainably sourced.
  • Fragile accessories are bubble-wrapped and placed in reinforced secondary packaging.
  • Gift orders include a complimentary ribbon and a personalised message card.
  • Cladly does not use single-use plastics in any stage of its packaging process.

International Deliveries

Cladly delivers to over 80 countries worldwide. International orders are dispatched from our fulfilment centre in Mumbai, India, and are handled by our trusted global logistics partners.

Customs & Duties: International shipments may be subject to import duties, taxes, and customs clearance fees levied by the destination country. These charges are the sole responsibility of the recipient and are not included in Cladly's prices or shipping fees.
  • Cladly declares accurate commercial values on all customs documentation.
  • We cannot mark international shipments as "gifts" to reduce customs liability.
  • Delivery timelines may vary due to customs delays — these are beyond Cladly's control.
  • Certain products (e.g., leather goods) may be restricted in specific countries — check local regulations before ordering.
  • International orders are not eligible for Same-Day or Next-Day delivery services.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can update your delivery address within 30 minutes of placing your order by contacting our support team. Once the order is processed and dispatched, address changes are no longer possible and Cladly cannot be held responsible for delivery failure due to an incorrect address.
Our courier partner will attempt delivery up to 3 times on consecutive business days. If delivery is unsuccessful after 3 attempts, the parcel will be returned to our fulfilment centre. You may request a re-delivery (additional shipping fee applies) or a full refund of the product value.
Please do not discard the original packaging. Photograph the damage and contact us within 48 hours of receipt via support@cladly.com or our WhatsApp helpline. We will arrange a replacement or full refund upon investigation. Damage claims raised after 48 hours may not be accepted.
Currently, Cladly does not ship to PO Boxes or APO/FPO addresses. A complete residential or commercial address with a PIN code and an active contact number is required for successful delivery.
Time-slot scheduling is available for Same-Day Delivery orders in select metro cities. After placing your order, our courier partner will send an SMS with a link to choose your preferred 2-hour delivery window, subject to availability.
