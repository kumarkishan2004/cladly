02

Order Processing

Orders placed on Cladly are processed on business days (Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays). Processing typically takes 1 business day before dispatch.

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM IST on a business day are processed the same day.
  • Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on Sundays are processed the following business day.
  • During sales events or festive seasons, processing may take up to 2 business days.
  • A dispatch confirmation email with your order status is sent as soon as your order ships.
03

Tracking Your Order

Once dispatched, you will receive an email with your order status updates.

  • Track your order at any time via My Orders in your Cladly account dashboard.
  • You'll get a notification at every stage — confirmed, packed, out for delivery, and delivered.
  • If your order shows no movement for more than 48 hours, please contact our support team.

Our Packaging

At Cladly, packaging is an extension of the product. Each order is carefully wrapped and sealed in our signature black-and-gold branded packaging.

  • We're actively working towards 100% recyclable packaging across all orders.
