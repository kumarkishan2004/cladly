Who We Are

A brand built by people who actually wear what they sell

Cladly is a fashion and lifestyle brand redefining everyday luxury for a generation that refuses to choose between style and substance. We started in college dorm rooms and group chats — frustrated by accessories that looked cheap, broke fast, or cost more than a month's allowance.

So we built something different: pieces designed with the same care as luxury houses, priced for the people who actually wear them every day — students, young professionals, and anyone who believes good style shouldn't be a luxury reserved for a few.

Our Vision

Fashion that says something, without saying too much

We envision a world where accessories aren't an afterthought — where a pair of earrings or a bracelet can carry as much intention as the outfit it completes. Cladly exists to make that kind of thoughtful styling accessible, not exclusive.

We're building toward a future where quality, sustainability, and individuality aren't trade-offs against affordability — they're simply how we do business, every single day.

Our Journey

How we got here

The Idea

Cladly started as a frustration turned plan — too many accessory brands were either overpriced luxury or flimsy fast-fashion. We wanted to build the option that didn't exist yet.

First Collection

We launched with a small, carefully chosen catalog — earrings, jhumkas, and bangles — tested first on friends, then on campus.

Going Digital

We built our own platform from scratch, designed around how our customers actually shop — fast, mobile-first, and visually driven.

Today

We're growing one referral, one campus, one order at a time — staying true to the same promise we started with: real quality, honest prices.
