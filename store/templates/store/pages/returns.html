Return Policy Overview

At Cladly Fashion, we stand behind every piece we sell. If for any reason you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return eligible items within 7 days of the delivery date for a full refund, exchange, or store credit.

All returns are subject to the conditions outlined below. Items must be in their original, unworn, unwashed, and undamaged condition with all original tags, labels, and packaging intact.

Cladly Gold Members enjoy an extended 15-day return window and priority processing on all return requests, with refunds credited within 48 hours of warehouse receipt.

How to Initiate a Return

Returning an order with Cladly is a straightforward 4-step process.

1

Log In & Request

Go to My Orders in your Cladly account, select the order, and click "Request Return". Choose the item(s) and reason for return.

2

Schedule Pickup

Select a convenient date and time for free doorstep pickup. Our courier partner will collect the parcel within 2 business days.

3

Pack the Item

Place the item in its original packaging with all tags attached. Attach the prepaid return label sent to your email. Seal the package securely.

4

Receive Refund or Exchange

Once our team inspects the returned item and approves it (1–2 business days), your refund or exchange is processed within 5–7 business days.

No account? Email fashioncladly@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return to initiate the process manually.
04

Refunds

Once your returned item is received and inspected at our warehouse, we will notify you by email of the approval or rejection of your refund request.

  • Approved refunds are credited to the original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • UPI and wallet payments are refunded within 2–3 business days.
  • Credit / debit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 days depending on your bank's processing time.
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) refunds are issued as Cladly Store Credit or direct bank transfer (NEFT).
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to a Cladly error (wrong item, defective product).
  • Partial refunds may be granted where items are returned in a condition different from original.
05

Exchanges

Cladly offers free size and colour exchanges for all eligible clothing and footwear, subject to stock availability. If your preferred size or colour is unavailable, you will be offered a full refund or store credit.

  • Exchange requests must be raised within 7 days of delivery (15 days for Gold Members).
  • Each order is eligible for a maximum of one exchange. Subsequent exchanges are not permitted.
  • The replacement item is dispatched after the original item is picked up and verified.
  • Price differences in exchanges (e.g. for a different product) must be settled at the time of the request.
  • International orders are not eligible for direct exchange — a refund will be issued instead.
06

Defective or Wrong Items

If you receive an item that is defective, damaged in transit, or different from what you ordered, Cladly will cover the full cost of return and prioritise your replacement or refund.

Report within 48 hours of delivery by emailing fashioncladly@gmail.com with your order number and clear photographs of the defect or incorrect item. Claims raised after 48 hours may not qualify for a complimentary return.
  • Cladly will arrange an immediate free reverse pickup for defective or wrong items.
  • A replacement will be dispatched on the same day the return is picked up, if stock is available.
  • If the item is out of stock, a 100% refund including original shipping fees will be issued.
  • Manufacturing defects discovered after 48 hours but within 15 days are also eligible — subject to assessment.
07

Non-Returnable Items

The following items are strictly non-returnable and non-refundable under any circumstances, except in the case of a proven manufacturing defect:

  • Innerwear, lingerie, and swimwear (for hygiene reasons)
  • Custom-made, altered, or personalised items
  • Items marked "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable" on the product page
  • Clearance or outlet merchandise unless defective
  • Items without original tags, labels, or packaging
  • Items that have been worn, washed, or altered by the Customer
  • Gift cards and Cladly store credits
  • Fragrance and beauty products once opened
